Pricing info
Prices are calculated and display automatically. If you want to know the specifics, you can see them here.
Base prices are as follows:
Coins
€1 = 100 million
€2 = 250 million
€5 = 700 million
Players
€1 = 20 cards
€2 = 45 cards
€5 = 150 cards
€10 = 350 cards
Packs
€1 = 20 packs
€2 = 45 packs
€5 = 150 packs
€10 = 350 packs
The above amounts are the thresholds. If you are between these thresholds, a calculation is done based on the closest threshold below the amount you are ordering, then rounded up to the nearest euro. For example, let's say you want to order 240 cards. The closest threshold below it is 150 cards. You pay the price for 150 cards which is €5. Now there are 90 cards remaining. Normally this would cost you €4 to buy on its own (2×45 cards for 2×€2=€4). But since you are already ordering more than 150 cards, you pay €5 per 150 cards. Thus, these 90 cards will only cost you €5/150×90=€3. This brings you to a total of €8 for 240 cards.
